This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you visit our website (regardless of where you visit it from) and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our services to you, especially regarding corporate gifting, printing, and packaging solutions.

  • Identity Data: Includes names, usernames, and contact details (email addresses, phone numbers) used for placing orders and communication.

2. How We Use Your Personal Data

We use your data for the following primary purposes:

  • To fulfill and manage your orders for corporate gifts, printing, and packaging.

  • To administer and protect our business and this website (including troubleshooting, data analysis, testing, system maintenance, support, reporting and hosting of data).
  • To deliver relevant website content and advertisements to you and measure or understand the effectiveness of the advertising we serve to you.

3. Sharing Your Personal Data

We may share your personal data with the parties set out below for the purposes set out above:

  • Third-Party Service Providers: We may engage trusted third parties to perform functions on our behalf, such as processing payments, fulfilling orders, delivering packages, and providing IT services.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your data where required by law or judicial process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of BeanArts, our customers, or others.

4. Data Security

We have put in place appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used or accessed in an unauthorised way, altered or disclosed. Access to your personal data is limited to those employees, agents, contractors and other third parties who have a business need to know.

5. Your Legal Rights

Under certain circumstances, you have rights under data protection laws in relation to your personal data, including the right to request access, correction, erasure, restriction, transfer, and objection to processing. Please contact us if you wish to exercise any of these rights.
